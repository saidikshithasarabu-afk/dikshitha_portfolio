Description

This webpage represents a simple College Feedback System designed to collect feedback from students. The page starts with a header that displays the title of the system. Below the header, a form section is provided where students can enter their details and give feedback about their experience. The form includes fields to enter the student's name and email address, which help identify the person giving the feedback. It also provides options for selecting the student's department and rating the overall experience. Students can choose their department from a list and select a rating such as excellent, good, average, or bad based on their opinion. In addition, there is a space where students can write detailed comments or suggestions. At the bottom of the form, there are buttons that allow the user to submit the feedback or reset the form if they want to clear the entered information. The page is also connected to an external stylesheet that helps in styling and organizing the layout to make the form look neat and user-friendly.
